DPI Is One Of The Best Polytechnic Institute In Bangladesh

Daffodil Polytechnic Institute is one of the best sister concerns of Daffodil Family. Daffodil Polytechnic Institute Originate from Bangladesh Skill Development Institute(BSDI) that start the journey since 17 April 2003.Daffodil Polytechnic Institute affiliated by Bangladesh Technical Education Board (BTEB), Ministry of Education at 2006 and start the 4 years diploma course Computer Technology, Telecommunication Technology, Electrical Technology, Civil Technology, Textile Technology, Garments Design and Pattern Making Technology, Architecture & Interior Design Technology, Tourism And Hospitality Management. Dream Entrepreneur Dr. Md. Sabur Khan, Chairman, Daffodil Polytechnic Institute & Daffodil Family think about the Technical Education development in Bangladesh and start the Modern Polytechnic Institute for the betterment of our young people. Now, Daffodil or Daffodil Polytechnic Institute is one of the Top Private Polytechnic Institute in Bangladesh. After Completed the 4 years Diploma Students or Alumni of Daffodil Polytechnic Institute can Easily Apply to Admit One of the Best Privet University in Bangladesh Daffodil International University.
